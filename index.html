Weitong Cai

London QMUL UESTC, NCTU Multi-Modal Learning Computer Vision

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London (QMUL), supervised by Prof. Shaogang Gong. I received my master's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Jianwen Chen. I also completed my Bachelor's degree at UESTC.

My research spans across the field of computer vision and machine learning, especially multi-modal representation learning.
